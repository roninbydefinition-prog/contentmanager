## ‚ú® Overview

This project is an advanced **AI Content Manager** designed to maximize content performance across multiple platforms (e.g., YouTube, TikTok, Blogs, X/Twitter). It goes beyond basic analytics by providing **proactive, personalized content advice** based on real-time niche trend analysis and deep auditing of the user's existing work.

The core goal is to give creators the edge by identifying viral opportunities and optimizing their current strategy for maximum viewer acquisition and retention.

* **Viral Niche Trend Analysis:** Scans multiple niche categories to identify trending topics, formats, and keywords that are currently gaining traction, minimizing research time.
* **Content Audit & Improvement:** Studies a creator's existing content library to pinpoint weaknesses (e.g., title effectiveness, content structure) and suggests specific, actionable improvements.
* **AI-Powered Promotion Strategy:** Generates optimized promotional copy and suggested distribution times for social channels to increase content visibility and attract new, relevant viewers.
